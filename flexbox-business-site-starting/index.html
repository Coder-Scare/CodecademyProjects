About Psychedelics

Psychedelics Anonymous is high quality concept art for those with fine tastes. The collection has a wide variety of unique traits and clothing. We have a mixture of suit and hoody Genesis art. The Genesis is the core to this collection which showcases the exuberant craftmanship that went into creating each piece with such detail. Owning a Genesis is more than just art. It is a tightknit community with likeminded individuals. Have a taste, become Psychedelic.
